I am an incoming PhD student in the Computer and Information Sciences department at the University of Pennsylvania. I am broadly interested in designing algorithms for problems in graph theory, geometry, and machine learning, particularly for modern computational settings (ex. local, learning-augmented).

I graduated from Columbia University, majoring in Computer Science and Mathematics. In Summer 2023, I participated in the DIMACS REU, in which I studied the hardness of approximation of diameter clustering. As an undergraduate, I was lucky to have wonderful mentors in Josh Alman, Karthik C. S., and Rocco Servedio.

I am supported by an NSF Graduate Research Fellowship.
