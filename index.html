Ashwin Padaki

I am a senior at Columbia University majoring in Computer Science and Mathematics. I'm broadly interested in theoretical computer science, particularly algorithms in online, stochastic, streaming and resource-constrained settings. In Summer 2023, I participated in the DIMACS REU, in which I studied the hardness of approximation of the diameter clustering problem.

I previously interned as a quantitative trader at Optiver, an options market making firm. In my free time, I play soccer and table tennis, and I enjoy watching most sports. I've recently gotten more into hiking and I'm trying to get better at composing minimalist piano music.
